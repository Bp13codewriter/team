When Subaru Natsuki leaves the convenience store, the last thing he expects is to be wrenched from his everyday life and dropped into a fantasy world. Things aren't looking good for the bewildered teenager, however not long after his arrival, he meets a mysterious beauty named Satella, in hot pursuit after the one who stole her insignia. Subaru offers to help in her search, and later that night, he even finds the whereabouts of that which she seeks. But just minutes after locating the insignia, Subaru and Satella are brutally murdered. However, Subaru immediately reawakens to a familiar scene; meeting Satella all over again. The enigma deepens as history inexplicably repeats itself.

Natsuki Subaru is the main protagonist of the Re:Zero. He is a shut-in NEET who is sent to a fantasy world. Subaru has short black hair and brown eyes. He is described to have an ordinary look and an evil gaze due to his sanpakugan. He usually wears the tracksuit that he was wearing when he was summoned.

Emilia is the main female protagonist of Re: Zero. She is a beautiful Half Elf with long silver hair and purple blue eyes. She wears a white and purple outfit along with a white flower in her hair. Emilia is good natured and likes to take care of others, though Emilia herself refuses to admit it, instead thinking of it as her own selfishness.

Rem is one of the twin maids working for Roswaal L Mathers. Rem has medium length sky blue hair that covers her right eye, large light blue eyes, and young features. She also has hair clips towards left side of her hair, flower-shaped ribbon on the same side of her hair, and a maid hairband.

Reinhard van Astrea is the current Master Swordsman and a member of the Royal Guard, currently acting as Felt's knight. Reinhard is mentioned to be a "perfect" person, friendly to others, clean, brave, and is of justice. He is known as a "knight among knights" and is widely trusted among the people of Lugnica.
